# Summary Report: 20/6

### Event Overview
- The event features 8 participating teams, divided into 4 rounds. The first round consists of 4 matches, with 2 teams competing against each other, each choosing a set of questions from the program.
- In each match, the two teams will answer 10 questions with increasing difficulty. A correct answer earns 10 points, while an incorrect answer deducts 10 points.
- Each round will showcase interesting skills such as: double points for correct answers but double points for incorrect answers, and the ability to answer incorrectly without losing points...
- To increase the excitement and drama of each round, the program will gradually increase the difficulty of the questions, and points will be added or deducted accordingly.
## Connect and exchange
- While the teams are exchanging ideas, those below can volunteer to answer the question and give hints for their answer. Those who answer correctly will receive attractive prizes from the program.
## Event Experience
- After participating in the event, I was able to learn a lot about things I had never learned before and improve my listening skills.
